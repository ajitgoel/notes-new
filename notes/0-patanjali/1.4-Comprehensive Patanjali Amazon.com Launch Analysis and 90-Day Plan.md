## Section 3: Detailed Unit Economics & Budget Allocation

### 3.1 COGS & Fee Assumptions

**Landed COGS Calculation:**

- **Base Procurement Cost (India)**: Assume 35% of Amazon.in retail (manufacturer-direct pricing advantage)
- **Freight (Air)**: $2.80/kg for supplements/personal care; $1.90/kg for foods (slower sea freight)
- **US Customs Duty**: 0-6.5% depending on HTS code (most ayurvedic supplements duty-free under HTS 1211.90; cosmetics 0-6.5%)
- **Customs Broker & Clearance**: $125 flat + $0.15/unit
- **Domestic Inbound to Amazon FC**: $0.35-0.60/unit depending on weight/distance

**Amazon Fee Structure (2024 rates):**

- **Referral Fee**: 15% for supplements/personal care; 8% for grocery/foods
- **FBA Fulfillment Fee**: $3.22-$5.85 based on size tier (most SKUs fall in standard/small standard)
- **Monthly Storage**: $0.87/cubic ft (Jan-Sep), $2.40/cubic ft (Oct-Dec) — factor into 90-day model
- **Advertising (PPC)**: Conservative 16% ACOS assumption for launch phase (industry avg 12-20% for supplements)

## Section 6: Compliance, Risk & Logistics Deep-Dive

### 6.1 FDA & Import Compliance Checklist

**Dietary Supplements (15 SKUs):**

- ✅ **FDA Registration**: File Facility Registration (CFR Title 21, Part 111) - $0 cost, online
- ✅ **Labeling**: Supplement Facts panel (not Nutrition Facts); must list all ingredients by weight
- ✅ **Structure/Function Claims**: Use permissible claims ("supports immune health") NOT disease claims ("cures diabetes")
    - **Compliant**: "Ashwagandha supports stress management and energy*"
    - **Non-Compliant**: "Treats anxiety and chronic fatigue"
- ✅ **GMP Compliance**: Verify Patanjali India facility has GMP certification (request cert copy)
- ✅ **Proposition 65 (CA)**: Test Shilajit and heavy-metal-risk herbs for lead/arsenic; include warning if >0.5 mcg/day
- ❌ **High Risk**: Avoid any SKUs with ephedra, kava, or restricted botanicals (none in current list)

**Cosmetics (6 SKUs - Face Wash, Aloe Gel, Shampoo):**

- ✅ **VCRP (Voluntary Cosmetic Registration)**: File with FDA; not mandatory but adds credibility
- ✅ **Ingredient Transparency**: Full INCI list on label (not just marketing names)
- ✅ **No Drug Claims**: "Anti-aging cream" = drug claim; use "nourishing cream for mature skin"
- ✅ **Preservative Safety**: Verify parabens/formaldehyde releasers comply with FDA limits

**Foods (4 SKUs - Ghee, Honey, Chyawanprash, Juices):**

- ✅ **Prior Notice (PN)**: File with FDA 2-8 hours before arrival at US port (via customs broker)
- ✅ **FSVP (Foreign Supplier Verification)**: Verify Patanjali has HACCP or equivalent safety system
- ✅ **Labeling**: Nutrition Facts panel mandatory; allergen declarations (milk for ghee)
- ⚠️ **Honey Tariff**: May face anti-dumping duty if sourced via China/India intermediaries; verify origin
- ⚠️ **Juices Shelf-Life**: Amla Juice 1L has 9-month shelf life post-opening; FBM only to avoid stranded inventory
