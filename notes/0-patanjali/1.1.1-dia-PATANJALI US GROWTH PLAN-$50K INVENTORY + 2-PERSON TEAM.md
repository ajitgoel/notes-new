**Compliance checklist (FDA/FTC, copy-safe)**

- Labeling: Supplement Facts panel, ingredients, warnings, manufacturer details, lot/expiry, GMP facility. No disease claims.
- Structure/function claims templates (copy-safe):

- “Supports immune health with vitamin C and zinc.”
- “Helps maintain normal energy metabolism with vitamin B12.”
- “Supports management of everyday stress.”
- “Provides antioxidant support with curcumin.”
- “Supports normal iron levels.”
- “Supports overall vitality with moringa.”

- Disclaimers:

- “These statements have not been evaluated by the Food and Drug Administration. This product is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease.”
- FTC: truthful, non‑misleading advertising; substantiation for any claims.

- Walmart/Amazon policies: comply with dietary supplement listing requirements; avoid prohibited phrases (“cures”, “treats”, specific diseases).
