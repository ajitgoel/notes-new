### **DECISION RULES: WHEN TO LAUNCH VS AVOID**

#### **Rule 1: Multi-Pack vs Singles**

**FAVOR MULTI-PACKS IF:**
- ✅ Product is daily-use consumable (toothpaste, shampoo, face wash)
- ✅ Competitor multi-packs rank higher than singles
- ✅ Subscribe & Save eligible (AWS >20% discount makes sense)
- ✅ Shelf-stable (12+ month expiry)

**FAVOR SINGLES IF:**
- ✅ First-time trial product (customers want to test before committing)
- ✅ Premium/niche item (specialized shampoo, anti-aging cream)
- ✅ Perishable or limited shelf life (<12 months)

**EXAMPLES:**
- Dant Kanti → **5-pack + 10-pack** (skip singles)
- Mukta Vati → **120ct only** (already bulk format)
- Aloe Vera Gel → **150ml single + 2-pack** (both)
- Chyawanprash → **1kg** (better value than 500g)

---

#### **Rule 2: FBA vs FBM/WFS**

**USE FBA IF:**
- ✅ Lightweight (<2 lbs), small (shoeboxshippable)
- ✅ High velocity (>5 units/day projected)
- ✅ Standardized packaging (no breakage risk)
- ✅ Prime badge critical for conversion

**USE FBM IF:**
- ✅ Heavy/bulky (ghee 1L = 2.5 lbs → high FBA fees)
- ✅ Perishable (juices, oils with <18 month shelf life)
- ✅ Low velocity (<3 units/day) = long-term storage fees
- ✅ High compliance risk (Divya Vati → isolate from FBA account)

**USE WFS (WALMART) IF:**
- ✅ Already in FBA but expanding channels
- ✅ Walmart approves seller (GMP cert required)
- ✅ Product has proven Amazon demand (don't test on Walmart)

**EXAMPLES:**
- Divya Mukta Vati → **FBM** (compliance firewall)
- Dant Kanti 10-pack → **FBA** (lightweight, high velocity)
- Ghee 1L → **FBM** (weight = $5.10 FBA fee vs $2.50 FBM shipping)
- Aloe Vera Gel → **FBA** (Prime badge essential for personal care)

---

#### **Rule 3: Subscribe & Save Eligibility**

**ENROLL IF:**
- ✅ Repeat purchase frequency <90 days
- ✅ Multi-pack format (encourages bulk commitment)
- ✅ Margin supports 15–20% discount (min 40% gross margin)
- ✅ Inventory forecast reliable (no stockout risk)

**DO NOT ENROLL IF:**
- ❌ One-time purchase (Mehndi hair dye, specialty churnas)
- ❌ Seasonal demand (Chyawanprash spikes in winter)
- ❌ Margins <35% (can't afford discount)

**EXAMPLES:**
- Dant Kanti 10-pack → **YES** (monthly use, 42% margin)
- Kesh Kanti Shampoo → **YES** (6–8 week repurchase)
