**POSITION SUMMARY**

This role architects and builds scalable backend systems that power pharmacy operations, manufacturing workflows, logistics coordination, and regulatory traceability. The Staff Software Engineer is a hands-on technical leader responsible for designing distributed systems that maintain high reliability under increasing operational demand. This position solves complex challenges across scalability, performance, and system resilience while establishing strong architectural patterns and engineering standards. The role partners cross-functionally to translate operational complexity into robust backend services and data systems that improve throughput, accuracy, and visibility. It also integrates artificial intelligence capabilities to enhance operational insight and engineering productivity. Success in this role means delivering resilient, high-performance systems that scale with business growth, enable rapid development, and strengthen Empower’s ability to operate efficiently in a highly regulated environment.
