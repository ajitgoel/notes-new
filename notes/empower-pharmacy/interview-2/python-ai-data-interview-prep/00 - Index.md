## How to Use This Vault

1. **Read each topic doc** — focus on concepts you're weakest in
2. **Try the exercises** in `11 - Practice Coding Exercises` *without* looking at solutions
3. **Check your work** against `12 - Practice Coding Exercises - Solutions`
4. **Use spaced repetition** — revisit topics every 2–3 days before the interview
5. **Tag your confidence level** on each topic: `#confident`, `#needs-review`, `#weak`
