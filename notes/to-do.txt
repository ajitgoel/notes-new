**Property Manager: High‑Level Responsibilities** 

*   **Maintenance, repairs & vendor coordination:**
    *   Proactively identify issues across common areas and units
    *   scope work
    *   schedule and oversee vendors for repairs (e.g., leaks, electrical, doors/windows, blinds, wall touchups, mailbox hardware)
    *   confirm completion and quality
*   **Turnover, marketing & unit readiness:**
    *   Ensure apartments are cleaned and made rent‑ready;
    *   paint unit walls or touchup unit walls as required.
    *   coordinate and obtain marketing photos;
    *   communicate status so units can be listed and leased without delay.
*   **Leasing documentation & move‑in/move‑out administration:**
    *   Collect signed leases/move‑in checklists;
    *   verify completeness
    *   send digital copy of lease document/move in checklist to owner to tenant as required.
*   **Rent, deposits & records reconciliation:**
    *   Collect rent/deposit payments
    *   verify amounts against lease terms;
    *   report variances and resolutions to ownership.
*   **Compliance, legal process & property oversight:**
    *   Act as the owners’ eyes and ears
    *   monitor ongoing issues (e.g., leaks, security/keys/mailboxes)
    *   support and track legal processes such as evictions
    *   conduct routine inspections
    *   provide timely updates, photos, and status reports to owners.
